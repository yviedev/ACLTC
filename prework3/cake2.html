How to Bake a Cake in 15 Minutes!

No, really...this is a real recipe that will save you lots of time.

By: Yvonne Ike

In other to bake this cake, you will need a few ingredients:

  1. Chocolate chips
  2. 1 egg
  3. 1 tsp of sugar
  4. 1 tsp of baking powder
  5. 1 cup of flour

You will also need a few pieces of hardware.

      1 ramekin
      (or microwave safe bowl if you want to be fancy.)
      A microwave

Step 1:

Start by mixing all of the above-mentioned ingredients together in your ramekin. You don't need a professional mixer -- just a metal fork with much hand power.

Mixing time: 5 minutes or until you get tired (or whichever one comes last.)

Step 2:

Lick the spoon. I assure you this is important for taste purposes. If your cake mix doesn't taste quite right, just improvise and a teeny weeny more of one or more of the above ingredients.

Step 3:

Wash your fork. I mean it's just one fork so far that you've used. Fine, drop the fork in the sink. Sheesh.

Step 4:

Plug in your microwave. See! That contraption would come in handy some day. Yes, microwaves are probably not great for your health overall, but neither is cake.

Step 5:

Stick your ramekin in the microwave for 5 minutes. Please do not stand or sit in front of the microwave while your cake is baking. Go do something else like...watch reality TV.

Step 6:

Let your cake cool down for a bit you cake monster. You've waited this long, you can afford to wait a little longer.
